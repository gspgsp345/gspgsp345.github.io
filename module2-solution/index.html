The Best Sports In The World

Hockey

Hockey is the first best sport in the world. Each team has 20 players that can play in a game. Usually, there's 12 forwards, 6 defenses and 2 goalies. There's 3 forwards, 2 defenses and 1 goalie at the same team on the ice in each team. The game last 3 periods of 20 minutes each. The goal is to put the puck in the other team's net.

Soccer

Soccer is the second best sport in the world. Each team has 18 players that can dress for a game. Usually, it's mixed up between defenses, midfielders, forwards and goalies. There's 11 players in each time on the field at the same time. The game last 2 half of 45 mimutes each plus some added time depending on the time they lost.

Tennis

Tennis is the third best sport in the world. You can play doubles or singles. Usually, players prefer to play singles if they're good enough to win prizes. You always play against another player or another doubles team. The game between 2 sets and 5 sets. To win a set, you have to win 6 games. The first player or team that wins 2 sets or 3 sets wins.
